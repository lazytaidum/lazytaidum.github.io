Development Timeline

A high-level overview of the project's development phases and estimated durations.

Phase 1: Core Mechanics & Prototyping

Duration: 1-2 Months

This initial phase is dedicated to building and validating the fundamental gameplay loop. The goal is to create a minimal but functional prototype to confirm that the core concept of building and automating is both fun and technically sound on the Roblox platform.

1. Player & World Setup

  • Standard Player Controller
  • Basic Inventory & Hotbar
  • Interaction Tool System
  • Simple Test Environment

2. The First Production Line (Core Loop)

  • Manual Resource Mining
  • Grid-based Placement System
  • First Machine: Smelter
  • Key Challenge: Conveyor Belts
  • First Automated Chain

Phase 2: Expanding the Factory

Duration: 2-3 Months

With the core loop proven, this phase introduces layers of complexity. The focus shifts from manual work to full automation, introducing more advanced machinery and the critical power grid system, forcing players to make strategic decisions about factory layout and resource management.

1. Automated Extraction

  • Mining Drill Mk.1
  • Enables full automation

2. Advanced Machinery

  • Constructor (1 input)
  • Assembler (2 inputs)
  • Robust Recipe System

3. Logistics Enhancements

  • Splitters & Mergers
  • Conveyor Lifts (Verticality)

4. The Power Grid (V1)

  • Machine Power Consumption
  • Biomass Burner Generator
  • Power Poles & Lines

Phase 3: Progression & The Open World

Duration: 3-4 Months

This phase focuses on giving players long-term motivation. The introduction of a research-based tech tree provides clear goals, while the expansion of the world encourages exploration to find rare resources and unique rewards, adding an element of adventure to the engineering challenges.

1. Research & Tech Tree

  • HUB / Research Terminal
  • Tiered Technology Unlocks
  • Core Progression Driver

2. World Expansion

  • Large-scale Biomes
  • Variable Resource Purity
  • Exploration Incentives

3. Basic Threats

  • Hostile Alien Fauna
  • Adds challenge to exploration
  • Basic Melee Weapon

Phase 4: Polish, Optimization & Pre-Launch

Duration: 2-3 Months

With all core features in place, the focus shifts to quality and performance. This phase is about transforming the functional game into a polished, seamless experience through UI/UX improvements, audio-visual enhancements, and critical performance optimization to ensure it runs smoothly for all players, even with massive factories.

1. UI/UX Overhaul

2. Sound & VFX

3. Optimization

4. Tutorial

5. Monetization

Phase 5: Launch & Post-Launch

Duration: Ongoing

The final phase covers the official release and the long-term commitment to the game and its community. This includes gathering feedback through testing, launching to the public, and establishing a cycle of continuous improvement with bug fixes, performance patches, and exciting new content updates.

1. Testing

  • Alpha/Beta Testing
  • Gather Feedback

2. Public Launch

  • Full Release on Roblox

3. Ongoing Support

  • Bug Fixes & Patches
  • Community Management
  • Future Content Updates
